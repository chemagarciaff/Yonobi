YONOBI PINCH POT CAFÉ

The Pinch Pot Café takes its name from the pinching technique, a simple method of shaping clay using only your fingers, without the use of a pottery wheel. At our Pinch Pot Café, the focus is on hand-building, where you can create small ceramics like bowls, cups, or sculptures at your own pace, guided by your creativity and our specially made tutorials.
